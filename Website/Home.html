Ten Facts about Opossums

click the picture on the right for more info

  • Opossums are marsupials found in the Americas.
  • They have prehensile tails.
  • Opossums are omnivores.
  • They play dead when threatened.
  • Females carry joeys in a pouch.
  • They are solitary and nocturnal.
  • Opossums have 50 teeth, more than any other North American mammal.
  • Opossums are immune to snake venom.
  • Fur color varies from grayish-white to black.
  • They are skilled climbers and nest builders.
